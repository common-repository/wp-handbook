Add wp-handbook in your website. Generate PDF of your favorites posts.

== Description ==

You can add posts in your handbook (favorites list) in order to print them or create a PDF.

== Installation ==

1. Upload 'wp-handbook.php' to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Configure your handbook in Setting and widget

== Screenshots ==

1. Link to add posts in your handbook
2. Link to view all of your posts
3. Your can re-organize your posts and make a title or an image
4. Edit your widget
5. Handbook Setting
